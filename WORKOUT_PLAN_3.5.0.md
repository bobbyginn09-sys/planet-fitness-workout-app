# NEXSET 3.5.0 — Five-Day Upper-Priority Plan

## Weekly structure

| Day | Session | Working sets | Cardio |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Monday | Upper Strength | 12 | Incline walk: 17 min, 2.8 mph, 6% |
| Tuesday | Lower Body | 15 | Incline walk: 12 min, 2.7 mph, 5% |
| Wednesday | Active Recovery | — | Easy walk: 25 min, 2.5 mph, 0% |
| Thursday | Push Build | 14 | Incline walk: 17 min, 2.8 mph, 6% |

## Starting weights and targets

### Monday — Upper Strength
- Smith Machine Bench Press — 3 × 8–10 at 135 lb
- Lat Pulldown — 3 × 8–10 at 85 lb
- Incline Dumbbell Press — 3 × 10–12 at 30 lb per dumbbell
- Seated Cable Row — 3 × 10–12 at 75 lb

### Tuesday — Lower Body
- Smith Machine Squat — 3 × 8–10 at 130 lb
- Smith Machine Romanian Deadlift — 3 × 8–10 at 140 lb
- 45° Leg Press — 3 × 12–15 at 120 lb
- Seated Leg Curl — 2 × 10–12 at 50 lb
- Leg Extension — 2 × 12–15 at 35 lb
- Calf Raise Machine — 2 × 12–15 at 60 lb

### Thursday — Push Build
- Machine Chest Press — 3 × 10–12 at 85 lb
- Machine Shoulder Press — 3 × 10–12 at 55 lb
- Machine Chest Fly — 2 × 12–15 at 60 lb
- Cable Lateral Raise — 2 × 12–15 at 2.5 lb
- Rope Triceps Pushdown — 2 × 12–15 at 25 lb
- Overhead Cable Triceps Extension — 2 × 12–15 at 10 lb

### Friday — Pull + Arms
- Chest-Supported Row Machine — 3 × 10–12 at 65 lb
- Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown — 3 × 10–12 at 75 lb
- Cable Face Pull — 2 × 12–15 at 15 lb
- Dumbbell Hammer Curl — 3 × 10–12 at 20 lb per dumbbell
- Cable Curl — 2 × 12–15 at 20 lb

### Saturday — Upper Definition
- Seated Cable Row — 3 × 10–12 at 90 lb
- Neutral-Grip Lat Pulldown — 3 × 10–12 at 75 lb
- Incline Dumbbell Press — 3 × 10–12 at 30 lb per dumbbell
- Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press — 2 × 8–12 at 25 lb per dumbbell
- Cable Curl — 2 × 12–15 at 35 lb
- Rope Triceps Pushdown — 2 × 12–15 at 20 lb

## Progression rule

Keep the listed weight until every working set reaches the top of its rep range with controlled form and no red/hard rating. Then use the app's normal next-weight increase. If any set falls below the minimum rep target or form changes, repeat the weight or reduce one increment.
